Minneapolis College of Art and Design MAGWD Capstone Project by Chrissie Nelson

ABOUT

Rebranding Especially for Children, which is an existing childcare organization with multiple locations in the Twin Cities area. Since EFC started in 1976, I believe a visual re-brand would be beneficial to both the teachers and parents at EFC and it would create a stronger brand identity overall. I want to create a simpler, more modernized look that incorporates new tools and systems to improve the EFC environment as a whole.
